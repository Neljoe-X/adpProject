THE COLOSSEUM

ARCHITECTURE

The Colosseum is an elliptical structure made of concrete and stone. It is 188 meters long, 156 meters wide, and 57 meters high. It could hold an estimated 50,000 to 80,000 spectators. The arena was covered by a retractable awning to protect spectators from the sun and rain.
