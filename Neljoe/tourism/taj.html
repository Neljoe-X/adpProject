TAJ MAHAL

ARCHITECTURE

The Taj Mahal, a breathtaking mausoleum in Agra, India, is a masterpiece of Mughal architecture. Its symmetrical design, intricate carvings, and white marble facade are a testament to the skilled craftsmanship of its time. The monument's central dome, flanked by four smaller domes and minarets, is a striking example of the fusion of Persian, Indian, and Turkish architectural styles. The surrounding Charbagh garden, a Persian-style paradise with four quadrants, further enhances the monument's beauty and serenity.

T0URISM

The Taj Mahal, a stunning mausoleum located in Agra, India, is a must-visit destination for any traveler. Its breathtaking beauty, intricate architecture, and rich history have captivated visitors for centuries. The best time to visit is during the cooler months of November to February when the weather is pleasant and crowds are manageable. The nearest airport is Agra Airport, and the nearest railway station is Agra Cantt. There are different ticket categories based on nationality, age, and whether you are a student or senior citizen. The Taj Mahal is open to visitors from sunrise to sunset. To make the most of your visit, wear comfortable shoes, carry water and snacks, avoid peak tourist season if possible, respect local culture, and consider hiring a guide to learn more about the monument's history and architecture. In addition to the Taj Mahal itself, Agra offers other attractions such as the Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri, and the Mehtab Bagh. Visitors can also enjoy local cuisine, shopping, and cultural experiences. The Taj Mahal is a truly unforgettable experience that will leave you in awe of its beauty and the love story that inspired its creation.
